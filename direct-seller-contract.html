Definition

In this Contract

  • "Direct Seller(s)" means a person authorized by a direct selling entity through a legally enforceable written contract to undertake direct selling business on principal-to-principal basis.
  • “Direct Selling Entity” means the principal entity which sells or offer to sell goods or services through direct sellers, but does not include an entity which is engaged in a pyramid scheme or money circulation scheme.
  • “Network of Sellers” means a network of direct sellers formed by a direct selling entity to sell goods or services for the purpose of receiving consideration solely from such sale.

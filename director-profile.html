Gopa DasGupta

(CEO)

Gopa DasGupta is the dynamic CEO of Arrishta Educart Pvt. Ltd., bringing over 18 years of experience as a PR professional with multinational corporations. Her expertise in public relations, combined with her profound understanding of psychology, has been instrumental in driving the growth and success of Arrishta. Gopa is a firm believer in the power of transparency and trust in business, which she considers essential for building strong and successful organizations. Her leadership style is characterized by a commitment to self-discipline, work ethics, and continuous personal and professional development.

Leadership Philosophy:

Gopa DasGupta emphasizes creating a company culture of openness, which she believes is more complex than merely complying with laws and regulations. She advocates for genuine trust as a force for good, asserting that transparency is key to building trust in business. With her background in psychology, Gopa encourages its application in business practices to foster growth, self-improvement, and team development.

Key Achievements:

Best CEO of 2023 - Honored by Direct Selling Today for her exceptional leadership in the industry.

Maharashtra Ratna Puraskar (2023) - Recognized for her significant contributions to the business sector.

"Swartna Stree 2023" Award - Celebrating womanhood, this prestigious award was presented to Gopa DasGupta for her outstanding achievements as an entrepreneur.

"Women Power" Achiever Award 2023 - Awarded the title of CEO of the Year (Female), recognizing her leadership and impact in the industry.
